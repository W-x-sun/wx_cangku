7 For our project we simulated and rendered hair in real-time. Features are listed below:
8 
9 ### Simulation
10 - Wind and gravitational forces
11 - Hair collisions with mesh
12 - Collisions and friction between hair
13 
14 ### Generating geometry
15 - Hairs are generated on the GPU from a small number of simulated guide hairs
16 - Hairs are turned into billboarded triangle strips
17 - Many OpenGL techniques used, including tessellation shader, geometry shader, and transform feedback
18 
19 ### Rendering
20 - Shadows using shadow mapping with percentage-closer filtering
21 - Shadows using deep opacity mapping
22 - Transparency using depth peeling
23 - Anti-aliasing using 4-sample supersampling-antialiasing
24 - Color variation between hairs
25 
26 ### User interface
27 - Interactive adjustment of many parameters
28 - Scene editor that allows user to draw custom hair placement and combing direction
